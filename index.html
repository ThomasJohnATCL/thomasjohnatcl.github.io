Research Interests

My core research focuses on improving the interpretability and alignment of deep learning architectures. Specifically, I work on justifiability in computer vision frameworks, aiming to align machine learning feature extraction mechanisms closely with human visual perspectives.

  • Current Focus: Vision Transformers (ViTs), Explainable AI (XAI), and custom evaluation metrics for validating neural alignments.
